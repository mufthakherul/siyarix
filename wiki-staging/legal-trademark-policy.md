# 🏷️ Trademark Policy

The Siyarix name and logo represent this project. This policy outlines how you can use the brand identity of this open-source tool.

## 📏 Scope of the Trademark

This policy protects:
- **The Word Mark:** "Siyarix"
- **The Logo:** The project logo.

## ✅ Usage Without Permission (Allowed)

I love it when the community talks about the project! You are free to use the Siyarix name **without asking** to:
- Refer to the project in text or code comments.
- Accurately state that your plugin "works with Siyarix."
- Comply with AGPL-3.0 attribution requirements.
- Write reviews, blog posts, or videos about the project.

## ❌ Usage Requiring Permission (Restricted)

To protect users from confusion, you need **explicit permission** to:
- Use "Siyarix" as part of your company name or product branding.
- Use the official logo as your own project's avatar.
- Register a domain name containing "siyarix".
- Imply that the Siyarix project endorses your product.

## 🔀 Forking & Attribution Rules

The AGPL license gives you the right to fork Siyarix. If you do:
1. **Rename It:** Please choose a new name to avoid confusing users.
2. **Be Clear:** State clearly that "This project is a fork of Siyarix."
3. **Change the Logo:** Do not use the official logo as your primary branding.
4. **Keep the License:** The AGPL-3.0 copyright notice must be preserved.

## 🏛️ Official Channels

To ensure you are downloading safe code, please use the official channels:
- **GitHub:** `https://github.com/mufthakherul/siyarix`
- **PyPI:** `https://pypi.org/project/siyarix/`

> [!WARNING]
> Any other website claiming to offer "Official Siyarix" should be treated with caution.

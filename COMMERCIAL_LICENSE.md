# Commercial Licensing for Siyarix

Siyarix is primarily distributed under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0)**. For organizations that cannot comply with AGPL-3.0 requirements, alternative commercial licenses are available.

---

## When You Need a Commercial License

You may need a commercial license if:

| Scenario | AGPL Compliance Challenge |
|----------|--------------------------|
| **Proprietary SaaS** | You integrate Siyarix into a closed-source SaaS product and cannot release your modifications under AGPL |
| **Embedded Systems** | You distribute Siyarix as part of a proprietary hardware or software product |
| **Enterprise Internal Tool** | Your legal policy prohibits open-source copyleft dependencies in internal tools |
| **OEM Distribution** | You bundle Siyarix with a proprietary product for redistribution |
| **Modified API/Services** | You offer modified Siyarix functionality as a network service and cannot publish internal improvements |

---

## What a Commercial License Provides

- **Proprietary use** — Use, modify, and integrate Siyarix without AGPL source disclosure obligations
- **Distribution rights** — Distribute modified versions under proprietary terms
- **SaaS deployment** — Offer Siyarix-powered services without disclosing modifications
- **Warranty** — Commercial terms include limited warranty and liability provisions
- **Support** — Priority support and maintenance commitments
- **Customization** — Access to maintainers for custom feature development

---

## How to Obtain a Commercial License

Contact the project maintainer:

- **Email**: Via GitHub profile (`mufthakherul`)
- **GitHub**: Open a discussion or issue tagged `commercial`

Please include:

- Organization name and size
- Intended use case and deployment model
- Estimated number of users or deployments
- Any specific requirements (support SLA, custom features, etc.)

---

## Pricing

Commercial licensing is negotiated on a case-by-case basis. Factors include:

- Organization size and type
- Deployment scope and user count
- Integration complexity
- Support requirements
- Revenue generated from Siyarix-powered offerings

**Free-tier**: Small teams, non-commercial research, and educational institutions may qualify for free or reduced-cost licensing.

---

## Relationship to Open Source

- The AGPL-3.0-licensed version remains fully open-source and free to use for all permitted purposes.
- Commercial licenses are additive — they provide additional permissions, not restrictions.
- We are committed to keeping Siyarix open-source long-term.
- Commercial revenue supports ongoing development, infrastructure, and community growth.

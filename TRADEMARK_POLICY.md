# Siyarix Trademark & Brand Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2026
**Version:** 1.0.0

This policy governs the use of the Siyarix name, logos, and related brand assets. It is designed to protect the integrity of the project while remaining open-source friendly.

---

## 1. What This Covers

The following are protected trademarks of the Siyarix project:

| Mark | Type |
|------|------|
| **Siyarix** | Word mark |
| **Siyarix Logo** | Design mark / logo |
| **Siyarix™** | Brand identifier |

These marks may only be used in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Permitted Use (No Permission Needed)

You may use the Siyarix name and branding in the following ways without prior approval:

- **Accurate attribution**: "Built with Siyarix" or "Powered by Siyarix"
- **Educational content**: Tutorials, articles, videos, and books about Siyarix
- **Community discussion**: Social media posts, forum discussions, conference talks
- **Fair references**: Referencing Siyarix in comparison with other tools
- **Integration mentions**: "Integrates with Siyarix" for compatible plugins or tools
- **Fork attribution**: Clearly labeling a fork as "Unofficial fork of Siyarix"

---

## 3. Use Requiring Permission

The following require explicit written permission from the project maintainers:

- Using the Siyarix logo as part of your product or company branding
- Using "Siyarix" in your product name (e.g., "Siyarix Enterprise" or "Siyarix Manager")
- Official partnership or certification badges
- Merchandise bearing Siyarix branding
- Domain names containing "Siyarix"

---

## 4. Prohibited Use

The following are **not permitted**:

| Prohibited Activity | Example |
|---------------------|---------|
| **Impersonation** | Distributing modified versions under the name "Siyarix" without clear differentiation |
| **Misleading branding** | Using Siyarix branding to imply official endorsement of unrelated products |
| **Fake official distributions** | Distributing modified code under the Siyarix name without indicating modifications |
| **Domain squatting** | Registering domains like "siyarix-security.com" without permission |
| **Trademark dilution** | Using the mark in a way that harms the reputation of the project |
| **Malicious forks** | Forking the project and using the name for malware, scams, or illegal activities |

---

## 5. Community Forks

We support the open-source ecosystem. If you fork Siyarix:

- **Do** rename your fork to avoid confusion with the official project.
- **Do** clearly state in your README that your project is a fork.
- **Do not** use the official Siyarix logo without modification.
- **Do not** imply your fork is endorsed or maintained by the original project.
- **Do** comply with the AGPL-3.0 license requirements.

Recommended forking attribution:
```
This project is a fork of [Siyarix](https://github.com/mufthakherul/phalanx).
It is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Siyarix project.
```

---

## 6. Official Channels

The only official Siyarix distribution channels are:

- **GitHub**: `https://github.com/mufthakherul/phalanx`
- **PyPI**: `https://pypi.org/project/siyarix/`
- **Docker**: Official images published by the Siyarix project

Any other distribution claiming to be "official Siyarix" should be treated with caution.

---

## 7. Logo & Visual Assets

- The official logo and visual assets are available in the project repository.
- Do not modify the logo for use in ways that could be confusing.
- Do not use the logo in a way that suggests endorsement without permission.

---

## 8. Reporting Violations

If you suspect trademark misuse:

1. Open an issue in the main repository (tag with `trademark`).
2. For urgent cases, contact the maintainers privately.

We will review reports and take appropriate action, which may include contacting the violator or reporting to domain registrars/platforms.

---

## 9. Enforcement

Violation of this policy may result in:

- A request to cease the infringing activity
- Public notice of unauthorized use
- Reporting to relevant platforms or registrars
- Legal action in cases of willful infringement or consumer harm

---

*This policy is designed to protect the Siyarix brand while fostering a healthy, open ecosystem. It is not intended to restrict legitimate discussion, education, or innovation.*
